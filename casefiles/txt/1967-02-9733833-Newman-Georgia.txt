11. BRIEF SUMMARY AND ANALYSIS
Observers watched a round shaped object with red and green lights flashing.  There was a halo about the object that flashed on and off irregularly.  Upon flashing on, the halo stayed on for about 2 seconds outlining and showing the metal skin of the object distinctly.

35.  Information which you feel pertinent and which is not adequately covered in the specific points of the questionnaire or a narrative explanation of your sighting.

When I first sighted the object I was in a door looking through window glass.  I noticed it shook with luminous disk notchy lined.  To make sure that it were a reflection off the glass I [went] outside and observed object.  There were an absence of sound from object.  There were red and green lights flashing on object, and they were constantly shifting positions.  There was a halo about the object that flashed on and off irregularly.  Upon flashing on the halo stayed on for about 2 to 3 seconds outlining and showing metal skin of object distinctly.

Being an Amateur astronomer I have observed aircraft, planetary movements, satellites, and meteors and under all conditions and none had borne any resemblance to this object.
