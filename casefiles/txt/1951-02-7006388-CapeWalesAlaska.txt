[Weather Station Wales, Alaska — UFO Report]

On 9 February 1951, personnel at the weather station at Cape Wales, Alaska observed unusual alternating red and green lights at bearing 242° (approximately SW) from the station at approximately 0305Z. The lights continued to be observed intermittently until approximately 0700Z.

The lights were observed to be stationary at the 242° bearing. The alternating red and green character of the lights and their apparent brightness attracted the attention of the weather station personnel.

Investigation determined that the lights were from a United States Navy icebreaker operating in the area. The vessel was using 24-inch searchlights to locate leads (channels) in the sea ice. The alternating red and green appearance was attributable to the ship's navigation lights and searchlight operations as the vessel worked through the ice.

No further investigation required.
