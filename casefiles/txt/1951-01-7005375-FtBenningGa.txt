Description of sighting:
On 12 January 1951 at approximately 1445 EST, nine (9) civilian observers at Fort Benning, Georgia observed an unidentified round object.

The object was described as round with a fan-shaped wake or trail behind it. The object remained stationary for approximately 20 minutes. After the 20-minute stationary period, the object departed rapidly to the southeast at high speed. No sound was reported.
